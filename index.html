Ciao! Welcome to my website

I am a NHFP Einstein Fellow at the Center for Astrophysics | Harvard & Smithsonian. I recently obtained my Ph.D. at Leiden University (see my thesis) working in the ENIGMA group of Prof. Joe Hennawi and in the galaxy-formation group of Prof. Joop Schaye. My research focuses on high-redshift quasars and the formation and evolution of supermassive black holes. I am also interested in cosmology and galaxy formation, and have worked on galactic outflows, gravitational waves, and protoplanetary disc morphology. Find out more about me and my research.
